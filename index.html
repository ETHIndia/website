Let's build decentralized applications

Decentralization is gaining momentum. In the coming years, emerging blockchain technologies like Ethereum will aid in distributing power from the center to the edges of our communities. This movement is capable to bring about the most significant technological and social advancements.

This hackathon is for all the developers and promising students who wish to contribute to our decentralized future. For the first time in India, ETHIndia brings together the curious minds, industry experts and companies to leverage and utilise the potential of Blockchain. We strive to bring in developers to participate in the hackathon irrespective of gender, race, sexual orientation, socioeconomic background or ethnicity. We’ll try to keep things simple.

FAQ

Everyone is welcome to apply, be it students, professionals or turing-test certified androids. If you are under 18 years of age, we’ll need a parental consent form.

We’re looking for people who "do"! Folks who are passionate enough to work on crazy world-changing ideas. Tell us what makes you apply to ETHIndia, what excites you, accomplishments you are proud of and whatever else you think can strengthen your chances of acceptance. We’ll get to know more about your abilities from the past projects, GitHub profile, participation/awards in other hackathons.

If you're looking to be a volunteer or mentor (i.e. someone with technical skills who'd be willing to help out participants during the weekend), you should reach out to us at mentor@ethindia.co. If your company is interested in becoming a sponsor, check out the sponsorship section below.
